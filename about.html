What is ClinicAssist?

ClinicAssist helps manage doctor's appointments. It is designed for two types of users:

Patients

Patients can book doctor's appointments using the ClinicAssist patient portal

Medical Office Assistants

Medical office assistants can approve or reject appointments using the ClinicAssist administrator portal.
