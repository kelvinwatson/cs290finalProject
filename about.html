What is ClinicAssist?

ClinicAssist helps manage doctor's appointments. It is designed for two types of users:

Patients

As a patient, you can book doctor's appointments using the ClinicAssist patient portal.

It's easy, just sign up, then request an appointment by filling out the request form. You can request as many appointments as you need.

Once requested, your appointment will be displayed as pending. A medical office assistant (administrator) will approve or reject your appointment request. Once approved or rejected, your appointment will appear in the approved or rejected section.

Medical Office Assistants

Medical office assistants can approve or reject appointments using the ClinicAssist administrator portal. Once you approve or reject an appointment, the corresponding patient will be able to see whether his/her appointment has been approved/rejected.
